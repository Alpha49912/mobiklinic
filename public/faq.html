  • Where do i find MobiKlinic Office/Headquater in Uganda?

    We have two locations, one in Kampala another in Buikwe. In Kampala, find us on Mawanda Road Plot 257. In Buikwe, find us in Bulyasi Kinoni Village Center;

  • How can I volunteer, partner, donate to or intern with Mobiklinic?

    Write us an email, we shall get back to you with an appointment or an email.

  • Who are the target beneficiaries of the services offered by MobiKlinic?

    Mobiklinic now has a model whereby every individual can benefit from our services irrespective of their financial status. Our work before was entirely on pregnant women and children

  • I am a doctor and I want to work with MobiKlinic. How does one get recruited or partner?

    Get in touch with us., we shall be happy to assistance you. You can send us a message via our official email address.

  • I have an idea to contribute to the improvement of healthcare access in marginalised communities. How can i contribute?

    Get in touch with us., we shall be happy to assistance you. You can send us a message via our official email address.

  • What are you social media pages?

    Follow us on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and linkedin.

  • What is the average bracket price/rate for Mobiklinic Services?

    1,000 Ugandan Shillings to 10,000 Uganda

  • Are the doctors in Mobiklinic qualified

    Mobiklnic is a platform working only with professional doctors and professionals from recognized universities in legally recognized hospitals and health centers.

  • How do we in the community identify a community health provider of Mobiklinic?

    The Mobiklinic makes an outreach to a community and call for a community gathering where the CHP gets introduced to the community.

  • When you call for the ambulance, do you pay it's fuel?

    Yes, you pay. Mobiklinic has organized free van treatment days. So, you wait for those days if you have no money. Also when you call a health center ambulance, you pay for it.

  • What is the area of operation of Mobiklinic

    Mobiklinic operates in Buikwe district for the start and plans to expand nationwide.

  • How can i get in touch with Mobiklinic if i am interested in expanding services to to my area?

    Please email (infor@mobiklinic.com) or call (+256700218686) to get in touch we shall get back to you on way forward.
